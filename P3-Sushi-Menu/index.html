Spiral Ecstasy Roll

  • Shrimp
  • Crab
  • Avocado
  • Salmon
  • Yellowtail
  • Tuna

The Spiral Ecstasy comprised shrimp, crab and avocado inside of the roll, and then alternating layers of tuna, salmon and yellowtail draped over the top. What makes this roll stand out is that each piece, theoretically, tastes a little different. It was fresh and light, with the manageable size of the pieces being a welcome plus. There was a brightness to the flavors that was enhanced nicely by the soy and wasabi, and countered with pungent scallion.
